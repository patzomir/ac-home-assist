# Market Analysis — Smart Heating for Bulgarian Apartments

## 🎯 Target Market

**Primary: Bulgarian apartment owners/renters (Sofia and other cities)**

- Split AC units are the dominant heating method in apartments
- Standard installation uses Schuko wall plug — smart plug monitoring viable without rewiring
- Rising electricity prices make cost visibility valuable even if savings are modest
- Tech-comfortable early adopters willing to pay ~220–250 BGN for a ready-made solution

---

## 🏆 Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Bulgarian market)

**None identified at this price point and apartment focus.**

### Indirect / Adjacent Competitors

#### SmartHomeSys.eu (Varna, Bulgaria)
- **Type**: Professional B2B/B2C integrator (founded 2008)
- **Primary platform**: Loxone — hub alone costs ~1,538 BGN, professional installation extra
- **Also sells**: Shelly, Fibaro, Energomonitor, Unitronics PLCs
- **Target**: Houses, offices, hotels, industrial facilities
- **Service model**: Quote-based design + installation + programming — not self-install
- **Scope**: Full BMS — lighting, HVAC, security, audio, access control
- **Conclusion**: Not a competitor. Different price tier, different customer, different complexity. Serves the market segment *above* this product.

#### International (not locally established)
- **Sensibo** — AC smart controller, cloud-based, subscription model, ~$99–149 USD, no local presence or Bulgarian support
- **Tado** — European smart heating, focused on gas boilers/radiators, not split AC units, not present in BG market
- **Home Assistant (DIY)** — Free, powerful, but requires significant technical setup; the idea replaces this for non-DIY users

---

## 📊 Market Gap

| Segment | Solution Available |
|---|---|
| Houses / offices — full automation | SmartHomeSys + Loxone (1,500+ BGN) |
| Tech enthusiasts — DIY | Home Assistant + Shelly (free + time) |
| **Apartments — plug-and-play, AC focus** | **Nothing established** ← opportunity |

---

## 💡 Strategic Position

- **Price point**: 220–250 BGN total kit — 6–7x cheaper than the Loxone hub alone
- **Self-install**: No electrician or integrator needed
- **Narrow focus**: Only AC heating — not a full BMS, which reduces complexity and cost
- **Local**: Bulgarian language, Bulgarian pricing (BGN), local support

---

## 🤝 Partnership Potential

SmartHomeSys resells Shelly devices — meaning if this product succeeds, there is potential for:
- Shelly as a shared component supplier
- SmartHomeSys as a referral channel for customers who outgrow this product

---

## ⚠️ Real Competitive Threats

1. **Shelly + Home Assistant** — the closest DIY alternative. Eliminates the gap if a non-technical user is willing to invest time. This product's value is saving that time and providing a focused UX.
2. **Sensibo expanding to Bulgaria** — possible but not happening today; their model is subscription-based which is a different trade-off.
3. **Chinese IR + WiFi plugs (Tuya ecosystem)** — cheap hardware exists, but no bundled software/dashboard for the Bulgarian energy cost context.

## 📈 Market Size Estimate (Bulgaria)

- ~1.5M apartments in Bulgaria, majority in Sofia, Plovdiv, Varna
- ~60–70% use split AC as primary or secondary heat source
- Early adopter segment (tech-curious, data-driven): estimated 2–5% of AC owners
- Conservative addressable market: **20,000–50,000 households**
- Initial realistic target (friends, network, Facebook groups): **15–30 customers**

---

## 🧭 Go-to-Market Strategy

1. **Phase 1 — Self**: Build and validate for personal use
2. **Phase 2 — Network**: Friends, family, Facebook groups (smart home / energy saving groups)
3. **Phase 3 — Word of mouth**: "You saved 87 BGN this winter" is the best ad
4. **Channels**: Facebook groups, direct referral, potentially tech blogs/forums

**Not planned**: Paid ads, retail distribution, marketplace listings — keep overhead zero until product is proven.
